# Examples Library

This file is a reference library of high-quality social media copy examples.
Claude uses these examples as qualitative benchmarks when writing new content.

**How to use this file:**

- Add your best-performing content here, organized by format
- The more specific and real the examples, the better Claude will calibrate to your style
- You do not need to fill every section — add examples only for formats you actually use
- Update this file over time as you find better examples

---

## How Claude uses these examples

When writing new content, Claude reads the relevant section and uses the examples to:

- Calibrate tone of voice and writing style
- Understand what "good" looks like for your specific brand
- Avoid outputs that feel generic or off-brand
- Match the energy, rhythm, and structure that works for your audience

The examples here take precedence over generic best practices.
If your style differs from a "standard" framework, your examples win.
