## Technical checklist before sending to designer

- [ ] Cover headline is specific — not vague or generic
- [ ] One idea per slide — no overloading
- [ ] Each headline works without the body text
- [ ] Slide numbers included where relevant
- [ ] Last slide has one CTA only
- [ ] Total slides within recommended range (4-12)
- [ ] No external links on slides (put in caption or first comment)
